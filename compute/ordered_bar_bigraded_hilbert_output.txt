  The bigraded generating function is:

    H(t, q) = sum_{n>=1, w>=1} dim B^{ord}_{n,w} t^n q^w
            = t * f_+(q) / (1 - t * f_+(q))

  where f_+(q) = ch(bar{A}; q) is the augmentation ideal character.

  Equivalently: H(t, q) = sum_{n>=1} f_+(q)^n t^n.
